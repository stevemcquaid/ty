Ty's Automative

Circle K at the Shell station (49th and Pennsylvania Streets) has plans to eliminate Tys Auto Service and Repair and to replace it with a convenience store. This is to petition Circle K to keep Ty and to not put in the convenience store. Convenience stores are magnets for crime (shop lifting and holdups), loitering, and added congestion.
