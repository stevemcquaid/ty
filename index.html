Ty's Automotive

Circle K at the Shell station (49th and Pennsylvania Streets) has plans to eliminate Ty's Auto Service and Repair and to replace it with a convenience store. This is to petition Circle K to keep Ty and to not put in the convenience store. Convenience stores are magnets for crime (shop lifting and holdups), loitering, and added congestion.

It’s time to rally around Ty! The stakes are high as Circle K makes its final decision. Ty’s lease expires this August and Circle K plans to continue with their redevelopment plans for a convenience store at the Shell gas station at 49 th and Pennsylvania St. If this plan goes through, Ty will be forced to vacate and we will be stuck with a convenience store that is a magnet for crime, loitering, traffic and more parking congestion. This will change the complexion of our neighborhood for years to come.

Ty’s Penn Shell Automotive Service is a small business that is neighborhood serving. “Ty is my go to …. The only reason I gas up there is because of Ty. Having Ty in the neighborhood truly makes this a special place. We could not be luckier to have him nearby.”
