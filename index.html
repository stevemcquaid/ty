Keep Ty's Automotive

Circle K at the Shell station (49th and Pennsylvania Streets) has plans to eliminate Ty's Auto Service and Repair and to replace it with a convenience store.

It’s time to rally around Ty! The stakes are high as Circle K makes its final decision. Ty’s lease expires this August and Circle K plans to continue with their redevelopment plans for a convenience store at the Shell gas station at 49 th and Pennsylvania St. If this plan goes through, Ty will be forced to vacate and we will be stuck with a convenience store that is a magnet for crime, loitering, traffic and more parking congestion. This would change the complexion of our neighborhood for years to come!

Ty’s Penn Shell Automotive Service is a small business that has been serving the neighborhood for 39 years!
